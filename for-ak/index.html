Benefits

Brain support

Compounds in blueberries may support memory and cognitive function.

Heart health

Antioxidants and fiber can contribute to healthy blood pressure and cholesterol.

Gut friendly

Blueberries provide prebiotic fiber that feeds beneficial gut bacteria.

Low calories

About 84 calories per cup with satisfying volume and sweetness.

Vitamin rich

Good source of vitamin C and K, plus manganese.

Antioxidant power

Anthocyanins help combat oxidative stress.

Easy recipe ideas

Blueberry smoothie

Blend blueberries, banana, yogurt, a splash of milk, and ice. Optional: spinach and chia.

Blueberry oatmeal

Top warm oats with blueberries, almond butter, cinnamon, and a drizzle of honey.

Summer salad

Spinach, blueberries, goat cheese, toasted almonds, and balsamic vinaigrette.
